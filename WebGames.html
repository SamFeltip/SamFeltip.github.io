Overview

During my time working on JavaScript applications, I have found it entertaining and productive for my coding practise and development discipline to rapidly develop small JavaScript Games.

These games are planned, written and tested in regular SCRMUS, to maintain my programming ability, to ensure I am continuously thinking of new project ideas, and because I enjoy it.

Multiplayer Snake

Use WASD, TFGH, IJKL, and the arrow keys to play 4 player snake on a single computer.

Snakes that are hit by other snakes will be split in two, and if they get to small, they die.

Snakes are randomly assigned colours and names.

Play Snake

2 snakes controlled by seperate players

When one snake crosses another snakes path, the snake that was crossed is split in two pieces.

Atari Breakout

Use the mouse to move the bar at the bottom of the screen. break all the blocks and dont lett the ball fall!

Watch out for the flashing bricks...

Play Atari Breakout

The Score is represented by the red number in the middle of the screen

Blue blocks increase the points, yellow blocks increase the platform size

Rainbow blocks make the game much harder!
