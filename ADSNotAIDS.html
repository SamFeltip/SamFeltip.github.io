ADS not AIDS

An automated disease survailance application

During my first year at the University of Sheffield, I was tasked to design a solution to an issue facing the community of Johannesberg, South Africa alongside a team of engineering students.

Our given task was Automated Disease Survailance, and so I designed an application which would allow residence to learn more about diseases in the local area, to input symptoms and recieve live medical advice, and to view past data on outbreaks to understand how viruses and other diseases spread.
