Timelines and Notifications

Timelines are collections of forms, and they can be associated with many study programmes


The following are variables which can be set for each form on a timeline

<%= icon('fa-solid', "calendar-day", class: "col-1 light fas fa-lg") %>
Due Days

A form submission is required after this many days

<%= icon('fa-solid', "door-open", class: "col-1 light fas fa-lg") %>
Open from

Decides how many days before deadline a form will open

<%= icon('fa-solid', "comment", class: "col-1 light fas fa-lg") %>
Reminders from

Decides how many weeks before deadline the student is notified

<%= icon('fa-solid', "clock", class: "col-1 light fas fa-lg") %>
Reminder Frequency

The student will be notified to submit the form frequently


Students can be manually notified by admins to fill in forms
This is done in the student view with the <%= icon('fa-solid', "comment", class: "light fas") %> icon

Study programmes

The base time limit of a study programme dictates the end of a students course

the "use default time limit" button implies the study programme's base time limit decides when a student graduates

When a student moves study programmes, their time limit is changed appropriately, but there is a manual override in the student edit view if needed


Research Groups

Research groups infer the primary department of a staff member

A staff member with the tutor rank can only act as a tutor on forms which are assigned to their primary department


Departments

Staff can be associated with many departments. This is done on the /myaccount view, or the staff view

It is extremely important forms, timelines, study programmes and research groups are assigned a department
If this is not done, these items may not be visible to the correct users
